Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I patent my college project?

Yes, if it is new and meets patent requirements. If your project contains a new mechanism or product build that doesn't exist publicly, it can be patented.

Can I publish and patent the same research?

Usually, the patent should be filed before public disclosure. You must file the patent application *before* you publish or present the paper publicly. Public disclosure kills your chance to secure a patent.

How much does trademark registration cost?

Explain government fees separately from professional fees. Government statutory filing fees are paid directly to the registry portal, while professional architecture and monitoring fees are kept as a flat transparent rate.
